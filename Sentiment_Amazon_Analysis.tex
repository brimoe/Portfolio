\hypertarget{introduction}{%
\subsubsection{Introduction:}\label{introduction}}

In this project, I produced a sentiment analysis covering over 500,000
reviews from an Amazon Fine Food Review dataset. I classified all
positive and negative customer reviews and then created word clouds,
plotly visualizations, and a text classification model to display my
analysis further.

\hypertarget{data}{%
\subsubsection{Data :}\label{data}}

For this project, I used the Amazon Fine Food Review
\href{https://www.kaggle.com/snap/amazon-fine-food-reviews?select=Reviews.csv}{dataset}
found on Kaggle.
